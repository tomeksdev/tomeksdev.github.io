1. Internal Cluster:
 - 2× HPE ProLiant DL380 Gen10 servers for virtualization.
 - 1× Synology SA3200D enterprise NAS.
 - 1× Synology UC3200 with RXD1219sas expansion for SAN deployments.
2. Client Hosting Cluster:
 - 4× HPE ProLiant DL380 Gen10 servers for hosting environments.
 - HPE MSA 2060 SSD SAS for high-speed critical workloads.
 - HPE MSA 2060 15K SAS for general-purpose VM storage.
3. Network and Security:
 - 3× Aruba 2540 switches for redundant network paths.
 - Sophos UTM SG210 for firewall, VPN, and traffic management.
